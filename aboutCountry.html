Incredible India

Incredible India, a country of vast contrasts, unimaginable diversity and splendid glory, offers a fascinating, exotic and exciting travel experience. Travelers get amazed and infatuated with the mesmerizing Taj Mahal (wonder of the world), astounding Forts & Palaces, ancient Temples, scenic Rajasthan Deserts, sun-kissed sea-beaches, Holy Ganges, backwater cruises of Kerala, breathtaking Himalayan Mountain peaks, astonishing wildlife including the Royal Bengal Tigers in their natural habitats, and much more.

THE TAJ MAHAL

One of the Wonders Of The World - The Taj Mahal is a white marble mausoleum that was built by Mughal emperor in remembrance of his beloved wife. It is situated on the southern bank of river Yamuna and is built on 17 hectares area and reaches 73 meters in height. Had this been built in today’s era it would cost US$827 million.

FORTS & PALACES

The majestic Forts & Palaces, built centuries ago and survived till date serve as reminders of the rich cultural heritage and glorious history when India was called a “Golden Bird”. The magnificent palaces amass the charming Hindu architecture and also the Mughal architecture, and boasts of opulent suites adorned with precious stones, and white/black marbles. The heavily guarded and rugged forts were designed as a defense mechanism against enemy attacks.

SNOWCLAD HIMALAYAS

The Tallest Mountain Range in the world attracts travelers of all genres. It offers diverse adventures including the exciting trekking, camping, hiking, paragliding, river rafting and nerve-wrecking winter sports, skiing, etc. which renders a rush of adrenalin. One can hop on scenic train rides; enjoy picturesque nature, feel divine peace, experience ancient healing science of Yoga and Ayurveda to bring together body, mind and soul.

BACKWATERS OF KERALA

With more canals than Venice, Kerala's emerald backwaters and houseboats offers tranquil excursions. Cruising past tiny villages and rich flora on-board house boats is an awe-inspiring experience. Golden sea-beaches, green mountain ranges, and powerful art-forms await to be explored.

CAMEL SAFARI & DESERT CAMPING

Enjoy the adventurous and world famous Camel Safari in the most colorful desert of the world – Thar Desert. Witnessing the beautiful sunrise and the sunset at Jaisalmer’s Sam sand dunes form the best excursion to explore. Enjoy staying in the luxurious desert camps, which are equipped with all modern amenities, traditional music & folk dance, and savory Indian cuisine.

ANCIENT TEMPLES

Ancient Indian temples, some standing at 243 ft and thousands of years old, are man-made wonders for their amazing chisel and engravings. Ajanta Caves dating back to 2nd century BC are rock cut cave monuments whose construction science is still a mystery. Spread over 45 acres, Meenakshi temple in one of the largest temples. Witnesssing the sexiest temple – Khajuraho Temple, which is both fascinating and admirable.

Facts about India

  • India is the most populous country on earth
  • Oldest civilizations of the world – INDUS VALLEY CIVILIZATION; 5000 years old Harappan culture in Sindhu Valley
  • The name 'India' is derived from the River Indus, the valleys around which were the home of the early settlers. The Aryan worshippers referred to the river Indus as the Sindhu.
  • Chess was invented in India.
  • Algebra, Trigonometry and Calculus are studies, which originated in India.
  • The 'Place Value System' and the 'Decimal System' were developed in India in 100 B.C.
  • Ayurveda - earliest school of medicine. Charaka established Ayurveda 2500 years ago.
  • Varanasi - the oldest inhabited city in the world – is the centerpiece of Hindu Culture
  • Yoga was originated in India 5000 years ago.
  • In India spirituality and religion are part of people’s everyday life.
